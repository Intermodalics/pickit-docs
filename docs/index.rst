Welcome to the Pickit knowledge base!
=====================================

Here you can find everything you need to know about
`Pickit <https://www.pickit3d.com/>`__, a plug and play 3D camera for your robot
that allows it to pick parts of different shapes, materials and sizes.
With Pickit you can automate any type of picking application such as bin picking,
machine loading, depalletizing, kitting and assembly, and pick and place.

Find out what's new in the latest :ref:`Pickit release <release-notes-2.2>`.

The panel to the left of the page guides you through the different topics of your
Pickit experience:

- **First steps** to get your Pickit system up and running.
- **Documentation** on how to use Pickit to detect your parts.
- **Robot integrations** describing the main robot brands Pickit can communicate
  with.
- **Hardware** specifications for our cameras and processor.
- **Examples** and exercises for learning from existing applications.

If you can't find an answer to your question here, please reach out to us by
sending an email to `support@pickit3d.com <mailto:mailto:support@pickit3d.com>`__.

**Happy picking!**
